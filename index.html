Evolutionary creatures (2018)

These are bio-mechanical creatures like organisms. Their appearance, shapes and behavior come from 3D simulate software EvoHandzel. They come from computer's quasi-physics environment. There are many factors responsible for evolutions. A special algorithm is still being discovered among scientists. „Evolutionary creatures” aren't perfect, because no organism will be perfect. They were into real world with minimal artificial intelligence (if it's classified this motion detection and reaction). There were created from the code. We can observe their behavior through eye contact. They detect our presence and express quasi-emotion or existence. This is a contact. Their first world was a virtual environment. The creatures inserted into glass domes just look preserved animals and plants in glass cylinders for scientific research because it’s a safe space for them and us. Will they work in this universe? Will the experiment be a failure like Frankenstein's monsters?

EvoHandzel Build 1.2 Alpha is fictional evolution's simulator software with using 3D and game engine which has basic laws of physics. It’s run in selected environment, which the user chooses. User build first shape of organism and initial some information. Software use too artificial intelligence to simulate new creatures. The organism evolved to selected time. The result is an 3D organism adapted to the given software’s environment.

Plant Wide Web (2018)

Plant Wide Web was inspired from Peter Wohllenben's book "The Hidden Life of Trees".

It is an interactive installation using Arduino modules connected to ultrasonic, gesture, light sensors and artificial plant forms. It's look a plant breeding. Bio-mechanical plants connected to a common network react to the presence of the viewer from close range by executing appropriate motion sequences. They work alone or in group.

Plants can create a huge underground network like Ethernet. Although we don't see that plants can respond, feel and communicate within network and the outside world. They work with other organisms such as insects and fungi. It's not entirely clear what mechanism is responsible for it. Although they do not have a brain or neural connections, they create a complex living system that may turn out to be a form of intelligence. Plants are aware that they are trapped in aquarium.

Displacement (2018)

The one balloon like boat or person. The balloons like boats, groups, society and people. If more they create large group and one shape. What is balloon? Last ago, it was used for transport and travel. They are thin and sensitive. One thing and air disappears. Can’t to be without air or gas. They haven’t any destination and use fate, because they have no will choose. The only strength is wind. They can easily change direction if someone use that. The balloons don’t have specific place, because they always in air and can fall into a trap. If the balloons are more, so they occupy more space. If we use shopping bags as balloons it reminds the consumer society. The white line is length of life or attachments to motherland, family, duties or friends. If they will lose their connection to the structure, they will fly away as free.

Fungi (2018)

Would you be surprised to know that the “original internet” is actually 1 billion years old? That’s right. The world wide web mimics the very first organism on earth – fungi.

The gallery space contain four corners. In one corner the artwork presents the mushroom like hub object. It is located like mold in a wet wall without anti-moisture isolation. The form is growing and expansion. Mushroom is not a plant. It is a strange organism. It isn’t animal too. It is located between. They grows faster than plants and have communication. They are surprising. For example there are mushrooms that change color, give a bad smell, release poison or the dust after an attack by an intruder. They are parasites, but some varieties can live in symbiosis with other organisms. In forests and places with a large number of plants and trees, mushrooms serve as a connection between plants and trees. They create internet like ancient modems (Wood Wide Web).

A very popular phenomenon in poorly constructed rooms susceptible to moisture is the expansion of fungus within the inner walls. Such a form can be deadly due to its carcinogenic nature. Very often they are located at the wall angles, where most often pipes with water or sewerage pass. Their expansion can cover the entire room.

You can connect with "Fungi" by phone or computer in Wi-Fi panel as ethernet device.
