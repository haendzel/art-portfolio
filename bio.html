About me

Filip Handzel (b. 1994, Cracow, Poland) is transdisciplinary artist and student from Jan Matejko Academy of Fine Arts in Krakow and Bezalel Academy of Arts in Jerusalem. He focuses on contemporary media arts including creative coding, robotics and installations. He participated in exhibitions and festivals in Czech Republic, Israel and Poland. In December 2018, he received a scholarship from the Minister of Culture in Poland for artistic achievements.
